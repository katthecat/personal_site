I am a 10th grader at Los Altos High School who's passion lies in idea development and engineering.

At school, I am involved in many activities and extra curriculars.

Currently, I am a member of the Los Altos Hacks III outreach team. My job is to help advertise the hackathon through various social media outlets. When needed, I also help our hackathon recruit sponsors. I am a part of the team because I first learned how much I enjoy programming at a hackathon during my freshman year. I just loved how coding gave you the ability to create literally anything you wanted to (including this website!). From then on, I have attended hackathons almost on a bimonthly-basis.

I am also the current treasurer of the Los Altos Hacks club on campus. Within the club, our goal is to educate and provide the community and resources necessary to foster a love of computer science among students on campus.

I am also the co-founder of a logic club on campus. The goal of this club is provide students with a place to work on their independent projects, where they can collaborate and participate in peer learning for the principles of logic. We will be using Arduino in order to learn these key electrical and computer science principles. More information will be given on this club as the school year starts.

Currently, I am working on a robotic cat toy that I can control via remote control.
